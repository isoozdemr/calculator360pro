## Blog SEO Optimization

### On-Page SEO

- **Title Tags**: 50-60 characters, include primary keyword
- **Meta Descriptions**: 150-160 characters, compelling
- **Headings**: H1, H2, H3 structure
- **Keywords**: Primary, secondary, long-tail
- **Internal Linking**: Link to relevant calculators
- **Content Length**: 1500-2000+ words
- **Images**: Optimized, alt text
- **Schema Markup**: Article schema

### Content Structure

```markdown
# Title (H1)

## Introduction
Brief overview, hook reader

## Main Content Sections (H2)
- Section 1
- Section 2
- Section 3

## Sub-sections (H3)
- Details
- Examples
- Tips

## Conclusion
Summary, call to action

## Related Calculators
Links to relevant calculators
```

## Internal Linking Strategy

### Link to Calculators
- Link to relevant calculators in blog posts
- Use descriptive anchor text
- Link naturally within content

### Link to Other Blog Posts
- Link to related blog posts
- Create topic clusters
- Build topical authority

### Link from Calculators
- Add "Related Articles" section
- Link to relevant blog posts
- Cross-link content
