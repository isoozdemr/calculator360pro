# Backlink Building Strategy

**Status:** Active  
**Last Updated:** 2026  
**Priority:** Critical

## Overview

Backlinks are one of the most important ranking factors for Google. This guide outlines strategies to build high-quality backlinks for Calculator360Pro.

## Why Backlinks Matter

- **Domain Authority**: Increases overall domain authority
- **Ranking Factor**: Direct Google ranking factor
- **Traffic**: Can drive referral traffic
- **Trust**: Signals trustworthiness to Google
- **Discovery**: Helps Google discover new pages

## Backlink Quality Guidelines

### High-Quality Backlinks
- ✅ Relevant to calculator/finance/health/education topics
- ✅ From authoritative domains (DR 30+)
- ✅ From trusted sources (educational, government, industry)
- ✅ Natural, editorial links
- ✅ Contextual placement
- ✅ Do-follow links

### Low-Quality Backlinks (Avoid)
- ❌ Link farms
- ❌ Paid links (unless disclosed)
- ❌ Spam directories
- ❌ Irrelevant sites
- ❌ Low-authority sites (DR < 10)
- ❌ Excessive exact-match anchor text

## Strategy 6: Broken Link Building

### Process

1. **Find Broken Links**
   - Use tools like Ahrefs, SEMrush
   - Check competitor backlinks
   - Find broken resource pages

2. **Identify Replacement Opportunities**
   - Find relevant broken links
   - Check if your calculator fits
   - Contact site owner

3. **Outreach**
   - Point out broken link
   - Offer your calculator as replacement
   - Provide value

### Outreach Template


## Tracking & Monitoring

### Tools

- **Google Search Console**: Monitor backlinks
- **Ahrefs**: Track backlinks, domain authority
- **SEMrush**: Monitor backlink growth
- **Moz**: Track domain authority

### Metrics to Track

- **Total Backlinks**: Growth over time
- **Domain Authority**: Improvement
- **Referring Domains**: Number of unique domains
- **Anchor Text**: Diversity and relevance
- **Link Quality**: DR/DA of linking domains

## Best Practices

1. **Quality Over Quantity**: Focus on high-quality links
2. **Natural Anchor Text**: Vary anchor text naturally
3. **Relevance**: Target relevant sites
4. **Patience**: Link building takes time
5. **Consistency**: Regular outreach
6. **Follow-up**: Follow up politely
7. **Track Results**: Monitor backlink growth
